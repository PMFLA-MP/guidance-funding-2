How Credit Education works

Our specialists review your credit profile (personal and business), identify issues and opportunities, and create an action plan. From there, we provide educational resources, guide you through dispute processes for inaccurate items, and help you establish trade lines that build business credit over time.

Who it's best for

  • New business owners building credit from scratch
  • Owners recovering from past credit issues
  • Businesses wanting to qualify for better rates
  • Applicants who've been turned down by banks
  • Anyone wanting to understand the credit system

Requirements

  • Current credit report access
  • Willingness to follow a 90-day+ plan
  • Business entity set up properly (or willingness to set one up)

Advantages

  • Qualify for better funding terms
  • Separate personal and business credit
  • Save on interest across all future financing
  • Protect yourself against identity and credit errors
  • Understand how lenders actually evaluate you
