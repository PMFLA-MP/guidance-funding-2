Purchase Order Financing

Purchase order (P.O.) financing pays your suppliers directly so you can fulfill customer orders that are too large to fund from working capital. Once the order ships and the customer pays, the financing is settled. It's the bridge between winning a big order and delivering it.

Funding amount
Up to $10,000,000
Term length
Transaction-based
Rate / cost
2–4% of PO value per 30 days
Time to fund
Setup 5–10 days; deals fund same-day once approved
Best for
Wholesalers, distributors, and importers, Government contractors fulfilling large orders, Manufacturers with supplier payment requirements

How Purchase Order Financing works

You receive a confirmed purchase order from a creditworthy customer. Guidance Funding's P.O. financing partner pays your supplier directly (via letter of credit or direct payment). You fulfill the order and ship to your customer. When your customer pays, the financing is settled and you keep the margin.

Who it's best for

  • Wholesalers, distributors, and importers
  • Government contractors fulfilling large orders
  • Manufacturers with supplier payment requirements
  • Resellers who don't carry inventory
  • Businesses with large orders from creditworthy customers

Requirements

  • Gross margin of 20%+ on the transaction
  • Creditworthy end customer (commercial or government)
  • Non-perishable, finished goods (typically)
  • At least 6 months in business
  • Confirmed purchase order or contract

Advantages

  • Fulfill orders larger than your working capital allows
  • No collateral beyond the order itself
  • Doesn't add debt to balance sheet long-term
  • Grows your business without equity dilution
  • Can be combined with invoice factoring for full cycle coverage

Frequently asked questions
