Equipment Financing & Leasing

Equipment financing lets you purchase the machinery, vehicles, or technology your business needs without tying up working capital. The equipment itself serves as collateral, which typically means easier qualification and better rates than unsecured loans.

How Equipment Financing works

Guidance Funding arranges financing for the equipment you need, paying the vendor directly. You take ownership of the equipment and make monthly payments over the term. Because the equipment acts as collateral, approvals are often faster and rates more favorable than unsecured options.

Who it's best for

  • New or used equipment purchases
  • Commercial vehicles and trucks
  • Restaurant equipment and kitchen buildouts
  • Manufacturing and construction machinery
  • Medical and dental equipment
  • Technology, software, and IT infrastructure

Requirements

  • Minimum 6 months in business
  • Monthly revenue $10,000+
  • Credit score 600+ (some programs go lower)
  • Equipment quote or invoice from vendor
  • Business bank statements

Advantages

  • Up to 100% financing including soft costs
  • Equipment serves as collateral — easier approval
  • Preserve working capital and other credit lines
  • Potential tax benefits (Section 179)
  • Fixed payments over equipment's useful life
