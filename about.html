Capital with conviction.

We built Guidance Funding because business owners deserve better than rigid bank formulas and one-size-fits-all rejections.

Since 2020, we've funded over $5.9 billion to more than 12,000 small and mid-sized businesses across every industry and all 50 states. But the numbers are only part of the story.

We started with a simple conviction: the financing industry was broken for the businesses that need capital most. Banks approve fewer than 20% of small business loan applications. Alternative lenders often hide their real rates behind confusing fee structures. And small business owners are expected to decode it all on their own.

We changed that by building a network of 75+ vetted lender partners and pairing every applicant with a real, U.S.-based funding specialist who advocates for their deal from application to closing.

How we're different

We shop the market for you. Instead of one rigid underwriting model, we match your application against 75+ lenders simultaneously — competing for your business.

All credit profiles welcome. We have funding for business owners with credit scores as low as 500. Bad credit, prior bankruptcy, tax liens — we've seen it all, and we have products for nearly every situation.

Real humans who know business. Our 100+ funding specialists aren't call-center scripts. Most have 10+ years of lending or small-business experience and understand the real-world cash flow issues behind your application.

No upfront fees, ever. No application fees, no origination fees charged by us, no hidden costs. You only pay when you're funded and using the capital.
