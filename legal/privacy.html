Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

At Guidance Funding, we take your privacy seriously.

1. Information We Collect

When you apply for funding or contact us, we collect:

  • Personal information: Name, email address, phone number.
  • Business information: Business name, state, time in business, industry, monthly revenue.
  • Financial information: Funding needs, credit profile (with your consent).
  • Technical information: IP address, browser type, device info, and usage data via cookies.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Evaluate your funding application and match you with appropriate lending partners
  • Communicate with you about your application, products, and services
  • Process transactions and provide customer support
  • Improve our website and services
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Sharing Information

  • Lending partners: To evaluate your application and provide funding offers.
  • Service providers: Third parties that help us operate our business.
  • Legal authorities: When required by law.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

4. Credit Reporting

By submitting an application, you authorize us and our lending partners to obtain consumer and business credit reports. Initial reviews typically use soft inquiries that do not affect your credit score.

5. Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to improve user experience, analyze traffic, and support marketing. You can disable cookies through your browser settings.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information — including encryption in transit (TLS), access controls, and regular security reviews.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your state, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your information
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Opt out of the sale of personal information (California residents)

8. Children

Our services are not directed to individuals under 18.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically.

10. Contact

Email us at admin@guidancefunding.com.
